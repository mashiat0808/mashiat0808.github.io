Welcome to my hobbies page!

As a child, I developed a passion for solving problems and have always been drawn to challenges. This love for problem-solving eventually led me to discover sudoku, a puzzle game that has captivated me for years. I have even won awards for my skills in sudoku, which has only further fueled my love for this game.

In college, my interests expanded to include photography and event management. I enjoyed the creative aspect of photography and the thrill of coordinating events. While I may not be a professional in these areas, they have remained a passion of mine and I continue to pursue them in my free time (the shot of my beautiful department building is taken by me by the way!).

In my free time, I also enjoy drawing and playing music on my guitar (even though I'm not particularly skilled at it). I find that both of these hobbies allow me to express my creativity and relax.

As technology continues to advance, I find myself increasingly interested in exploring new technology and its various applications. I enjoy learning about the latest gadgets and software and finding new ways to use them.

Finally, if I had it my way, I would ditch traditional schooling and instead focus on self-study and research. I have always been a fast learner, especially when it comes to subjects that I am passionate about, and I believe that self-guided learning can be a very rewarding experience.

In conclusion, my hobbies and passions are diverse and varied, but they all bring me joy and allow me to express myself in unique ways. Thank you for visiting my hobbies page.
